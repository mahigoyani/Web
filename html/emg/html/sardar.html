statue of unity

Height: 182 m Created: 31 October 2013–October 2018

The Statue of Unity is the sculpture of Iron Man of India ‘Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel’. It is situated in the Gujarat province of India confronting the Sardar Sarovar Dam. The Statue of Unity is all around perceived as the ‘World’s tallest sculpture’. The sculpture was initiated on 31st October 2018 by the Prime Minister of India. The task was reported by Narendra Modi on seventh October 2010 when he was the Chief Minister of Gujarat. The Statue of Unity was worked with an expense of about INR 2989 crore. Approximately 3000 specialists and 300 architects were utilized in the undertaking to finish. The sculpture is made with bronze cladding and surrounding of steel which is fortified by cement and metal covering. The Statue of Unity took right around 4 years to be designed. The figure of the sculpture was structured by Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri Recipient Ram V Sutar.

The Statue of Unity is the world's tallest statue, with a height of 182 metres, located near Kevadia in the state of Gujarat, India. It depicts Indian statesman and independence activist Vallabhbhai Patel, who was the first deputy prime minister and home minister of independent India and an adherent of Mahatma Gandhi.
